Four Classic Warcraft Recipes

CLAMBAR

Clambar provides you with a buff for 20 stamina and spirit for 30 minutes. The item is created with Cooking (300); taught by [Recipe: Clam Bar], a Sporeggar reputation reward.

Materials Required:

  • 2x [Jaggal Clam Meat]

That's it! That's the only ingredient! Happy clamming!
